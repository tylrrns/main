3D Carotid Bifurcation

Software: Ansys Fluent
Governing Equations: Continuity Eqn, Navier-Stokes Eqns
Boundary Conditions: Inlet: 0.315 m/s steady velocity, Outlet: 100 mmHg (13332 Pa) static pressure, Wall: No-slip
Simplifying Assumptions: Isothermal, incompressible flow; Blood density ρ = 1060 kg/m3, μ = 0.0035 kg/m-s
Mesh: 27453 nodes, 87540 elements
