3D Bifurcating Artery (Steady)

Ansys Fluent was used to model steady blood flow as it moves from the common carotid artery into the internal and external carotid arteries.

Governing Equations: Continuity equation (steady-state) and Navier-Stokes equations.

Boundary Conditions: No-slip, inlet velocity is steady at 0.315 m/s (Re = 600), outlet pressures are static at 100 mmHg (13,332 Pa).

Add'l Assumptions: Blood density = 1060 kg/m3 and dynamic viscosity = 0.0035 kg/m-s.

Mesh: 27,453 nodes, 83,709 elements.

Solving For: Pressure and velocity fields, wall shear stress.
