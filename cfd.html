3D Bifurcating Artery (Steady)

Ansys Fluent was used to model blood flow through the carotid bifurcation. A steady-state flow was assumed. The continuity equation and Navier-Stokes equations—simplified for steady flow—governed this system. No-slip condition was specified, as was an inlet velocity of 0.315 m/s (Re = 600), and outlet pressures static at 100 mmHg. Blood density was 1,060 kg/m3, dynamic viscosity, 0.0035 kg/m-s. The mesh comprised 27,453 nodes and 83,709 elements. Numerical solutions for pressure and velocity, wall shear stress (WSS) are given in Fig. 1 and 2.
